About Me

In addition to my technical skills, I bring strong problem-solving, teamwork, and communication abilities, which I have developed through leading projects, mentoring peers, and tutoring as part of my GloS Coaching initiative. I’m eager to tackle any challenge because I believe in using technology to empower others, promote digital inclusion, and support education-focused causes that foster innovation and growth.
